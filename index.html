Overview

About LKQ India

LKQ India Pvt. Ltd. is a fully owned subsidiary of Keystone Automotive Operations Inc., part of the LKQ Corporation family — a Fortune 500 global leader in alternative and specialty parts for automobiles and other vehicles.

Established in Bengaluru in 2008, we have grown into a team of 1600+ highly skilled professionals across Technology, Finance, Analytics, Shared Services, Cybersecurity, and Supply Chain — delivering world-class solutions that power LKQ's global objectives across North America, Europe and beyond.

Our Journey

LKQ India established in Bengaluru as a Global Capability Centre to support LKQ Corporation's growing international operations.
2012
Expanded to 500+ employees across Technology, Finance, and Shared Services functions.
2018
Crossed the 1000-employee milestone. Recognised as a Top GCC Employer in India.

Certified Great Place to Work® for two consecutive years. Team grows to 1600+ professionals.

Toastmasters

Find Your Voice at LKQ India

Our Toastmasters club empowers every employee to become a confident communicator and inspiring leader — one speech at a time.

LKQ India Toastmasters Club

Chartered in 2016, our club has grown into one of Bengaluru's most active corporate Toastmasters chapters — helping hundreds of members conquer the fear of public speaking, sharpen their leadership skills, and build lasting confidence.

What?

A structured programme of prepared speeches, impromptu speaking (Table Topics), and evaluation that builds communication skills progressively.

Why?

Because strong communicators make stronger teams. Our members report greater confidence in meetings, presentations, and leadership roles.

How?

Weekly meetings every Wednesday, open to all LKQ India employees. No experience required — just the willingness to grow.

Impact

200+ members, 15+ District-level award winners, and countless personal breakthroughs since our founding in 2016.
