Overview

Who we are

LKQ India Pvt. Ltd. operates as a fully owned subsidiary of Keystone Automotive Operations Inc., a proud member of the LKQ Corporation family. Established in 2008, our Bengaluru-based operations have grown to a team of 1600+ highly skilled professionals, driving innovation and delivering scalable solutions that support LKQ's global objectives.
